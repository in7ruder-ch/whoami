Cloud-Deployed ELK Stack Lab

Honeypot Project

Raspberry Pi & T-Pot 22.04

I developed a honeypot test lab on a Raspberry Pi to analyze TTPs (Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures) associated with intrusion attempts. The lab is directly connected to the internet and regularly receives real attack attempts, allowing for detailed analysis of the methods used by potential attackers.
